About Us

We are a government-funded college specifically for high-demand trades and technical careers. Our goal is to address housing insecurity by giving opportunities for more skilled workers in the workforce. By offering cheap training with direct pathways into essential and high-paying jobs, financially struggling individuals can quickly enter the workforce as a valuable member and hopefully afford stable housing. Our solution restores independence and dignity for unhoused individuals, rather than having them rely on temporary shelter systems. It also supports labour shortages in healthcare, trades, and infrastructure, industries Canada is already desperate to fill.
